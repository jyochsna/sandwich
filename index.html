Steps to make sandwich

  1. take two slice of bread on a plate
  2. Insert spreading knife on mayonise jar and load desired amount onto knife. spread onto one side of a sliced bread
  3. choose meat and place onto one of the pieces of bread
  4. place desired amount of cheese you have chosen on top of the meat
  5. place desired toppings (sliced tomatoes, pickle, lettuce)on top of your cheese
  6. place the second slice of brad on top of your toppings.
